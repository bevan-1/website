About Us

Anti Slip Floor Safety Pty Ltd is a proudly Australian-owned company with over 35 years of experience helping homes and businesses prevent accidents before they happen.

Our mission is to provide simple, effective, and long-lasting anti-slip treatments tailored to each client's unqiue needs.

Your floors will only be treated by our fully trained and experienced Application Team (covered with Public Liability and Product Insurance - for your added protection).

Anti Slip Floor Safety Pty Ltd is the manufacturer as well as the applicator of the treatment. With many years of experience in sucessfully treating slippery floors, you can be assured of a high level of service and the best results in improved slip resistant safety.
